ABOUT US

Our Story

In 2009, we set up our very first store in Gandhi Bazaar, Bengaluru with one vision: With an efficient team delighting customers and driving growth through unique products and initiatives, we have grown to 110 outlets, spread across the continent.

World Bean Coffee had its humble beginnings in one of the traditional coffee growing districts of Karnataka.

Uncompromising in Quality

Quality of ingredients is what sets great food apart from good food. Which is why we handpick our ingredients so that we can guarantee a top-tier culinary experience.

Green Initiatives

We understand that taking care of Mother Earth is our responsibility and have initiated several green practices keeping this in mind. We serve filter coffee in earthen pots and use biodegradable cups and plates. Used coffee grounds are packed and given away at our stores.

Social Responsibilities

Since the beginning of our journey, we strongly believed in creating equal opportunities for everyone. We are proud to have senior citizens and specially-abled team members as part of our workforce and culture.

From bean to cup

We take care of the whole process, from picking of the ripest mountain coffee cherries to the roasting of the beans for the best tasting cup in the world.
